⚡ Smart Energy Saving System

Reducing Electricity Waste Using Motion Sensors and Automation

About the Project

The Smart Energy Saving System automatically controls lights and fans using motion sensors. It helps save electricity by switching OFF appliances when no one is present in the room.

Problem Statement

In homes, hostels, classrooms, and offices, lights and fans are often left ON unnecessarily, leading to energy wastage and higher electricity bills.

Project Objectives

  • Reduce electricity wastage
  • Automate control of appliances
  • Lower electricity bills
  • Promote sustainable energy usage

How It Works

  1. Motion sensor detects human presence.
  2. Controller receives sensor data.
  3. Lights and fans turn ON automatically.
  4. Appliances turn OFF when room becomes vacant.
